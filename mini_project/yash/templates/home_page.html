WHAT ARE GANs?

GANS stands for Generative Adverseserial Network

Brief Introduction

The concept of GANs, is we have 2 neural networks, generative and Discriminative where the generative model,takes the input, process every inch of the pixels,based on weight and with the help of the trained dataset it, was trained on it generates different,output. The discrimimator on the other hand,is trianed on the real images as the input to it,so whenever,genrator creates an image,discriminator's job is to discard all the fake image.This image creation and discarding continues until the genrator makes such a image,that makes discriminator believe is true.
